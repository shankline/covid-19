Coronaviruses (COVID-19) are a large family of viruses that are known to cause illness ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS).

This webpage let's you know about the virus, the symptoms, prevention and how safe you are.

Checklist
Symptoms Point
Do you have cough 1pt
Do you have colds 1pt
Do you have Diarrhea 1pt
Do you have Sore Throat 1pt
Are you experiencing body aches 1pt
Do you have headache 1pt
Do you have fever(Temperature 37.8℃ and above) 1pt
Are you having difficulty breathing 2pts
Are you experiencing Fatigue 2pts
Have you travelled recently during the past 14 days 3pts
Do you have a travel history to a COVID-19 Area 3pts
Do you have direct contact or is taking care of a positive COVID-19 PATIENT 3pts
Score Result
  • 0-2 may be stress related and observe
  • 3-5 Hydrate properly and proper personal hygiene. Observe and re-evaluate after 2 days
  • 6-12 seek a consultation with doctor
